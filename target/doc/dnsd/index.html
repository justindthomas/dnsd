imp-dnsd — DNS caching recursor + forwarder.

Runs as a supervised child of impd inside the dataplane netns. Every socket goes through VPP’s session layer via vcl-rs — no linux_cp TAP, no punt path, no kernel sockets.

Public surface is small by design; most types live in sub-modules and are wired together by main.rs.
